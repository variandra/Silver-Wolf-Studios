Silver Wolf Studios

My Name is River and I make Fursuits!

I'm an amature fursuit maker trying to start a bussiness as a full time maker and artist! I have been making suits for 5 years and drawing since I was young. I have an Associates in Graphic Design and love all things art.

Commission Status

Fursuits: Closed

Art: Closed

I am currently working on my queue and plan to open commissions once I finish it. I will only be taking a limited amount of commissions so make sure you keep an eye out for my next opening!
